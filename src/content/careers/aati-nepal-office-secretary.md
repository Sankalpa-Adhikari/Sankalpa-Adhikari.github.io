## About Organization
Association of Agricultural Technology Industry Nepal (AATI Nepal) works on technology, agricultural mechanization, seeds, fertilizers, post-harvest by providing training and services to all the small to large scale industrialists and businessman.

## 🔧 Responsibilities
- Manage bilingual correspondence by drafting and responding to official emails and letters in both English and Nepali.
- Support agricultural projects by organizing technical data related to seeds, fertilizers, and mechanization services.
- Maintain organized filing systems for both physical and digital documents to ensure efficient information retrieval.
- Coordinate office schedules by managing appointments, meetings, and travel arrangements for senior staff with farmers and industrialists.
- Record detailed meeting minutes and distribute them to relevant team members for follow-up actions.
- Perform high-speed data entry and document preparation using Nepali Unicode and English word processing tools.
- Oversee office logistics including inventory management, procurement of supplies, and equipment maintenance.
- Facilitate internal communication between departments to ensure smooth operational workflow.
- Ensure data confidentiality by handling sensitive company information and records with high discretion.
- Prepare periodic reports and presentations as required by management.

## 🧠 Requirements
- Bachelor in Agricultural Engineering / Agricltural Science or equivalent
- Minimum of 2 years of relevant work experience
- Bilingual proficiency in English and Nepali with strong typing skills in both languages.
